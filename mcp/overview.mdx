Cobalt MCP empowers **AI agents** with instant access to enterprise integration actions across **Salesforce**, **NetSuite**, **SAP**, and **200+ platforms**.

Execute **one-off integration actions** in real-time without building and deploying specific workflows.

<Info>
MCP gives AI agents the ability to call integration logic as part of prompts or conversations. The API exposes **JSON Schema specifications** for comprehensive capabilities across your enterprise technology stack.
</Info>

## Use Cases

<Tip>
Real-world examples of how Cobalt MCP transforms AI agent capabilities across business functions.
</Tip>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Salesforce CRM Operations">
    **Intelligent Lead Management**
    - Create **leads and opportunities** based on AI conversation insights
    - Automatically capture **prospect information** and qualify leads
    - Initiate **follow-up processes** from customer interactions
    
    **Example:** During customer support, AI agent identifies upsell opportunity and creates qualified lead in Salesforce with conversation context.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="NetSuite Financial Management">
    **Real-Time Financial Operations**
    - Query **real-time financial data** and budget information
    - Generate **purchase orders** from demand forecasting analysis
    - Access financial insights and execute **procurement processes** instantly
    
    **Example:** AI agent analyzes inventory trends, forecasts demand, and generates purchase orders while providing budget impact analysis.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="SAP Enterprise Resource Planning">
    **Automated Resource Management**
    - Check **inventory levels** across multiple warehouses
    - Update **material master data** automatically
    - Manage complex **supply chain operations** with precision
    
    **Example:** During production planning, AI agent checks inventory across global warehouses, identifies shortages, and updates material requirements in SAP.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
