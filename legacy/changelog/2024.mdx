<Note>
Through 2024, Refold shipped on a monthly release cadence under an earlier versioning scheme, so these entries are grouped by month and labelled `2024.MM` for the period they cover. Unified (BOM) versioning with per-release entries began in February 2026.
</Note>

<Update label="January 2024" description="2024.01 · New Greenhouse">
