---
title: Create a Refold Developer Account
description: Create your Refold Developer Account, recieve your API Keys, and build and test on Refold.
---

To get started, contact our Support team [here](mailto:support@refold.ai?subject=Requesting%20Refold%20Developer%20Account) to get account of Refold to receive your test and production environment API Keys and use the Refold APIs.

1. Once you have received your login credentials and logged into Refold, you can find your API Keys.
2. Navigate to `Developer` in the side menu and under `Setup`, you can find you **Test** and **Production** API Key.
<img height="200" src="/images/implementation/api.png" alt="API Keys for your Refold account"/>

The Refold Dashboard is your centralized place to manage your Refold Applications, view Linked Accounts, review Logs, set up Workflows etc.
