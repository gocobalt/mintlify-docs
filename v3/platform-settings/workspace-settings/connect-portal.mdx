The **Connect Portal** is the public-facing portal your customers use to
link their accounts and manage their integrations. This page controls its
branding, which sections and workflow nodes customers can see, the AI
features available to them, and the legal and redirect URLs.

Open it from **Settings > Configuration > Connect Portal**, then click
**Save** to apply your changes.

## Design

Customize the look of the portal.

- **Theme** — `Auto`, `Light`, or `Dark`.
- **Color Palette** — the accent color used across the portal: Gray, Red,
  Pink, Purple, Cyan, Blue, Teal, Green, Yellow, or Orange.
- **Navigation** — where the portal navigation sits: `Top`, `Left`, or
  `Right`.
- **Collection** — how items are displayed: `List` or `Card`.

## Visibility

Toggle which sections and actions customers see in the portal:

- **Projects** — view projects and project workflows.
- **Application Workflows** — create private workflows for their connected apps.
- **AI Workflows** — create private AI workflows.
- **Execution Logs** — view execution logs for their private workflows.
- **Execution Log Details** — view the details of those logs.
- **Show Configs Switcher** — switch between multiple saved configs.
- **Show New Config Dialog** — create new configs from the portal.
- **Audit** — view audit logs.

### Hidden nodes

If customers build their own workflows, **Hidden Nodes** hides specific node
types from them — for example, **Rule**, **Delay**, **Loop**, **Tables**, or
**Transform**. The counter (such as `0 / 23`) shows how many are hidden.

## AI

Turn on AI-assisted features in the portal:

- **Workflow Agent** — let customers generate, edit, and test workflows with AI.
- **Field Mapper Agent** — let customers automatically map config fields with AI.

<Note>
  AI agents must be enabled for your organization first. **Contact support**
  to turn them on.
</Note>

## Portal display

- **Open Workflows in New Tab** — private workflows always open in a new tab.
- **Show Organization** — show your organization's information in the portal.
- **Show Linked Account** — show the linked account's information in the portal.
- **Remove Branding** — hide Refold's branding to white-label the portal.

## Experimental features

<Warning>
  These features are experimental and may be changed or removed in the future.
</Warning>

- **Pipelines** — let customers create pipelines.

## URLs

Configure the links the portal uses:

- **Redirection URL** — where customers return after they finish authenticating.
- **Terms of Service** — your terms-of-service link, shown to customers.
- **Privacy Policy** — your privacy-policy link, shown to customers.
