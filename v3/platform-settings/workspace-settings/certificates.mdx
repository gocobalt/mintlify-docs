The **Certificates** page holds the custom mTLS and inbound certificates
used by integrations that require certificate-based authentication. Upload
your certificates here so connectors can present and validate them when they
connect.

Open it from **Settings > Configuration > Certificates**.

## Certificate stores

Certificates are organized into three tabs, each for a different part of a
TLS handshake:

- **mTLS Trust Store** — the certificates Refold trusts when verifying a
  partner's server during mutual TLS.
- **mTLS Certificate Store** — the client certificates Refold presents to a
  partner during mutual TLS.
- **Server Trust Store** — additional certificate authorities Refold trusts
  for server TLS connections.

Each store lists its certificates with the **Common Name (CN)**, the date it
was **uploaded**, and the **expiry** dates so you can track upcoming renewals.

## Add a certificate

Click **+ Certificate** in the top-right and upload your certificate to the
relevant store. New certificates appear in the list with their CN and expiry.

## Download a CSR

Click **CSR** to download a certificate signing request. Have it signed by
your certificate authority, then upload the signed certificate back here.

<Warning>
  Refold doesn't auto-renew certificates. Track the expiry dates shown in
  each store and replace certificates before they lapse to avoid failed
  connections.
</Warning>
