---
title: "Policy"
description: "Set organization-wide security and data retention policies."
---

The **Policy** page is where admins set organization-wide rules, such as
requiring multi-factor authentication and how long logs are kept.

Open it from **Settings > Configuration > Policy**, then click **Save** to
apply your changes.

## Enforce MFA

Turn on **Enforce MFA** to require every user to use multi-factor
authentication to access the platform. Users who haven't set up MFA are
prompted to configure it before they can continue.

<Tip>
  Users set up MFA from their own [Authentication](/v3/platform-settings/personal/authentication)
  page.
</Tip>

## Data retention

The **Data Retention** section controls how long Refold keeps your
organization's data.

**Log Retention** sets the number of days logs are kept for your
organization (30 days by default). To change the retention period, contact
support.
