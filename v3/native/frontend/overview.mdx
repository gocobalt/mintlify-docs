An **auth flow** is how a customer authorizes a third-party app inside your product so Refold can act on their behalf. The flow runs in your **frontend**; once a customer connects, their credentials are stored against their [linked account](/v3/concepts/linked-account) and Refold signs every request for them.

This page is your starting point for building that experience in a Native integration. The connection flows themselves and connection management are documented once in the [Authentication hub](/v3/authentication/overview); this section covers what is specific to a Native frontend, namely embedding the flow with the React SDK and branding it. (How a *connector* authenticates, such as OAuth vs key-based and scopes, is set once on the connector; see [Authentication](/v3/connectors/app-auth-modes).)

## Before you start

Every flow needs two things in place on your **backend** first. Never expose your API key in the browser:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a linked account">
    Create one [linked account](/v3/concepts/linked-account) per customer with a stable ID. See [Create a linked account](/v3/api-reference/linked-accounts/create-linked-account).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a session token">
    Mint a short-lived [session token](/v3/api-reference/session-tokens/generate-session-token) for that linked account and pass it to your frontend. The flow uses it instead of your API key.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Choose a flow

All three flows do the same job, let a customer authorize an app, and differ mainly in how much UI you build:

| Flow | What it is | Build effort | Best when |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **[Hosted flow](/v3/authentication/connection-flows/hosted-flow)** | Redirect the customer to Refold's hosted portal | Lowest, no UI | You want connect + config handled for you |
| **[React SDK flow](/v3/authentication/connection-flows/react-sdk-flow)** | Drop Refold's connect component into your React app (no redirect) | Low, prebuilt components | You have a React app and want it in-product |
| **[Build your own](/v3/authentication/connection-flows/build-your-own)** | Build a fully custom UI with the JavaScript SDK | Highest, full control | You need a bespoke connect experience |

The React and build-your-own flows use the [client-side SDKs](/v3/api-reference/javascript-sdk); the hosted flow needs none.

## After a customer connects

Once an account is connected, the customer configures what the integration does through [config fields](/v3/native/configure/integration/config-fields), and you keep the connection healthy over time:

- **[Connection status](/v3/authentication/managing-connections/connection-status)**: check whether an account is connected.
- **[Re-authentication](/v3/authentication/managing-connections/re-authentication)**: prompt a reconnect when a connection expires or is revoked.
- **[Token refresh](/v3/authentication/managing-connections/token-refresh)**: how Refold keeps access tokens current automatically.

## Make it yours

Match the connect experience to your brand:

- **[Custom styling](/v3/native/frontend/customization/custom-styling)**: colors, naming, and layout.
- **[White-label flows](/v3/native/frontend/customization/white-label-flows)**: remove Refold branding from the experience.
