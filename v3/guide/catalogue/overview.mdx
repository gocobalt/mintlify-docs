Refold houses **over 300 connectors** — prebuilt integrations to third-party apps across CRM, accounting, HRIS, support, marketing, and more. The catalogue is where you browse them and open any app's **provider page**.

For what a connector is and the building blocks it gives you, see [Supported apps & actions](/v3/connectors/supported-apps-actions).

## Browse the catalogue

Open **Catalogue** in the sidebar and pick an app — they're listed alphabetically — to jump to its provider page.

## What's on a provider page

- **Authentication** — the method the app uses and what you configure to connect it. See [Authentication](/v3/connectors/app-auth-modes).
- **Actions & triggers** — the operations and events the connector ships with.
- **App-specific notes** — required scopes, account tiers, or setup quirks unique to that provider.

<Note>
Don't see the app you need? Build it as a custom app, or extend an existing connector with a [custom action](/v3/connectors/custom-actions).
</Note>
