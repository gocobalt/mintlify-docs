Your **profile** is the identity Refold uses for you across the platform:
your name, contact details, and the avatar that appears next to your actions
in audit logs and on shared workspaces. Everything on this page is scoped to
you, not to your workspace or organization.

Open it from the user menu in the top-right corner of the dashboard, or go
directly to **Settings > User > Profile**.

## Update your profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Profile">
    Go to **Settings > User > Profile**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your details">
    Update **First Name** and **Last Name**. The avatar in the right column
    rebuilds from your initials as you type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your phone number">
    Pick the country dial code (defaults to **US +1**), then enter the
    number without spaces or dashes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** at the top of the form. Changes apply immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Email address

Your email is the address you sign in with and where Refold sends account
notifications. A green **Verified** badge next to the email confirms you've
completed the verification flow.

To change the email on your account, contact your workspace administrator.
Email changes go through a verification step and can't be self-served from
this page.

## Avatar

Refold builds your avatar from the initials of your first and last name. The
preview on the right updates as you edit the name fields. Custom avatar
upload is not currently supported.
