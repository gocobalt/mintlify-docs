---
title: "Workflows"
description: "Automate what your integration does with workflows, built once on the platform and reused across your customers."
---

Workflows are the automations behind a native integration: triggers, nodes, data mapping, and the logic that moves data between your app and your customers' connected apps. You build them once on the platform and reuse them across every linked account.

Native does not redefine how workflows work. Build and configure them in the platform, then expose them to your customers from your [embedded UI](/v3/embedded/frontend/expose-workflow).
