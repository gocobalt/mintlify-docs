title: "Helm"
description: "Self-host Refold on your own Kubernetes cluster with the Refold Helm chart — you provision the cluster and operate the platform end-to-end."
---

**Helm** is the self-hosted, self-operated option: you run Refold on a
Kubernetes cluster you provision, in a connected environment, and operate it
end-to-end. Refold publishes the Helm chart; you install, configure, upgrade,
and monitor it.

<Info>
  **Which Kubernetes option?**
  - **Helm** *(this page)* — self-host and operate everything yourself in a connected cluster.
  - [**Air-gapped**](/v3/developer/deploy/kubernetes/air-gapped) — you own the cluster and network; Refold installs the app in a disconnected environment.
  - [**Refold Managed (BYOC)**](/v3/developer/deploy/kubernetes/refold-cp) — Refold provisions and manages everything in your AWS account.
</Info>
