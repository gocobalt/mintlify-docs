The **control plane** is the management layer that orchestrates Refold
deployments: it stores configuration, schedules workloads onto the data
plane, exposes the dashboard and API, and handles auth, audit, and
upgrades. The data plane (your workflow runners and connectors) executes
the work; the control plane decides what runs where and keeps everything
healthy.

You choose who operates the control plane based on your data-residency and
ops-capacity needs. The page you're on now is the architectural reference;
the deployment guides below cover how to install each option.

## Control plane options

You can run Refold's control plane in one of three modes. Pick the one that
matches your data-residency and ops requirements.

| Option | Who runs the control plane | When to pick it |
|---|---|---|
| **Refold Cloud** | Refold, in Refold's own infrastructure | Fastest path. No deployment work. Data plane can be Refold-hosted or self-hosted. |
| **Refold Managed** | Refold, inside your Kubernetes cluster | Data residency in your cloud account, without operating the control plane yourself. |
| **Self-Hosted** | You, inside your Kubernetes cluster | Full control over upgrades, networking, and the runtime. Highest ops burden. |
