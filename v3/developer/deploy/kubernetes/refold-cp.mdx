**Refold Managed** is the deployment option where you run Refold inside your
own Kubernetes cluster and Refold operates the [Control
Plane](/v3/developer/deploy/control-plane) for you. Your data stays in your
infrastructure; Refold handles the management, upgrades, observability, and
day-two operations.

Use this when you need data residency inside your own cloud account but don't
want to operate the control plane yourself. For the fully self-operated
option, use [Helm](/v3/developer/deploy/kubernetes/helm).
