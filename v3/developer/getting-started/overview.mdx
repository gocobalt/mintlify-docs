Refold lets SaaS companies offer third-party native integrations to their customers without building and maintaining each one in-house. A single installation connects your product to hundreds of popular apps, and Refold handles authentication, execution, and per-customer data isolation. Teams avoid the cost, time, and risk of building an integrations layer from scratch.
