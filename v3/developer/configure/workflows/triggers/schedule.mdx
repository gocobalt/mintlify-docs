---
title: "Schedule"
description: "Run a workflow automatically at a set time or on a recurring interval."
---

The **Schedule** trigger runs a workflow automatically at predefined times
or intervals — without any external event. It's ideal for recurring jobs
like sending regular reports or periodically syncing data.

## Schedule types

Refold supports two ways to define a schedule.

### CRON expression

Use a CRON expression when you want precise control over when the workflow
runs. The schedule is evaluated in **UTC**.

| Expression | Runs |
|---|---|
| `*/30 * * * *` | Every 30 minutes |
| `15 * * * *` | At 15 minutes past every hour |
| `0 9 * * *` | At 9:00 AM every day |

### Date / time intervals

Use this mode to run the workflow at specific dates and times. For each run,
choose a **date**, **time**, and **timezone**, then click **Add Date**. You
can add multiple run times. Click **Save** when you're done.

<Note>
  The minimum schedule interval is 5 minutes.
</Note>

Once deployed, the workflow runs automatically at the scheduled times for
every user who has enabled it.
