The **Email** page controls how Refold-sent messages appear to your
customers and teammates: the sender name and address, the reply-to address,
and the templates Refold uses for invitations, password resets, and
workflow notifications.

Open it from **Settings > Organization > Email**.

## Sender identity

Set the **From name** and **From address** that appear on every Refold
email. Customers see this on integration-related notifications.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Email">
    Go to **Settings > Organization > Email**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit sender details">
    Update **From name**, **From address**, and **Reply-to**. The address
    must be a domain you control.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the domain">
    Refold sends a verification email and lists the DNS records to add to
    your domain's TXT records. Add them and click **Verify**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. Refold uses the new identity on the next send.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Each Refold email type uses a template you can override. Pick a template
to edit the subject line and body. Refold renders the body with workspace
variables ({{`{{workspace.name}}`}}, {{`{{user.email}}`}}) at send time.
