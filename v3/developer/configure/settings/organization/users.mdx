The **Users** page lists everyone on your workspace and the role each
person holds. Workspace admins use it to invite new teammates, change a
teammate's role, and remove access when someone leaves.

Open it from **Settings > Organization > Users**.

<Info>
  You need the **Admin** role to invite or remove users. Editors can view
  the page but can't change membership.
</Info>

## Invite a teammate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Users">
    Go to **Settings > Organization > Users**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite">
    Click **Invite** in the top-right corner. A panel slides in for the
    invitee's details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their email and name">
    **Email** is required and is where Refold sends the invitation. **Name**
    is optional and seeds the invitee's profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invite">
    Click **Invite**. Refold emails the invitee a sign-in link and adds them
    to the user list with the **Editor** role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Roles at a glance

Every user holds one role. There's exactly one Admin per workspace; every
other user is an Editor by default.

| Role | What they can do |
|---|---|
| **Admin** | Full control over the workspace: invite and remove users, change roles, edit organization settings, manage billing. Only one Admin per workspace. |
| **Editor** | Build and modify integrations and workflows, manage linked accounts, view logs. Can't change workspace settings or membership. |

For the full role and permission breakdown, see [Roles](/v3/developer/configure/settings/organization/roles).

## Remove a teammate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the user">
    Locate the teammate in the user list on **Settings > Organization >
    Users**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Remove">
    Click the remove icon in the rightmost column of that user's row.
    Refold asks you to confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Remove**. Their access is revoked immediately. Active workflows
    they own keep running; ownership transfers to the Admin.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Removing a user is immediate and can't be undone. Re-add them by sending
  a fresh invite.
</Warning>
