The **Connect Portal** is the public-facing portal your customers use to
link their accounts and manage their integrations. This page controls its
branding, which sections and workflow nodes customers can see, the AI
features available to them, and the legal and redirect URLs.

Open it from **Settings > Configuration > Connect Portal**, then click
**Save** to apply your changes.

## Design

Customize the look of the portal.

- **Theme** — `Auto`, `Light`, or `Dark`.
- **Color Palette** — the accent color used across the portal: Gray, Red,
  Pink, Purple, Cyan, Blue, Teal, Green, Yellow, or Orange.
- **Navigation** — where the portal navigation sits: `Top`, `Left`, or
  `Right`.
- **Collection** — how items are displayed: `List` or `Card`.

## Visibility

The **Visibility** section controls which parts of the portal customers see
and what they can do with their private workflows. Each option is a toggle
with an inline description in the dashboard.

For example, you can let customers view projects and project workflows,
create their own application or AI workflows, see execution logs, switch
between saved configs, or view audit logs — and choose whether workflows
open in a new tab.

### Hidden nodes

If customers build their own workflows, **Hidden Nodes** lets you hide
specific node types from them — for example, **Custom Code**, **HTTP Node**,
or **Send Emails**. The counter (such as `0 / 23`) shows how many nodes are
currently hidden.

## AI

The **AI** section turns on AI-assisted features in the portal, such as
generating and editing workflows or automatically mapping config fields.

<Note>
  AI agents must be enabled for your organization first. **Contact support**
  to turn them on.
</Note>

## Experimental features

<Warning>
  These features are experimental and may be changed or removed in the future.
</Warning>

The **Experimental Features** section lets you opt into early functionality —
for example, allowing customers to create pipelines, showing organization or
linked-account details in the portal, or removing Refold's branding.

## URLs

The **URLs** section configures the links used in the portal: the
**Redirection URL** customers return to after authenticating, and your
**Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy** links shown to customers.
