The **Connect Portal** is the page your customers see when they authorize a
third-party integration through Refold. Use this settings page to match the
portal's look and the legal links to your own product, so the experience
feels like part of your app instead of a Refold redirect.

Open it from **Settings > Organization > Connect Portal**.

## Brand colors

Set the brand colors Refold uses across the Connect Portal. Two colors
matter:

- **Primary color** drives the dominant accent (active states, links,
  highlighted UI).
- **Button color** drives the action buttons your customer clicks to
  connect, authorize, and continue.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Connect Portal">
    Go to **Settings > Organization > Connect Portal**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the colors">
    Enter a **hex code** in each field or pick from the color picker. The
    preview at the right updates as you type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. Customer-facing sessions pick up the new colors
    immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Redirect and legal URLs

These three URLs control the customer's path out of the portal after they
finish connecting an integration, and the legal links shown inside the
portal.

| Field | Behavior |
|---|---|
| **App Redirection URL** | Where the customer's browser lands after a successful connection. Use a deep link into your app's "integration connected" screen. |
| **Privacy Policy URL** | Link Refold displays in the portal footer. Shows the customer where to read your privacy policy. |
| **Terms of Use URL** | Link Refold displays in the portal footer. Shows the customer your terms of use. |

<Tip>
  Use HTTPS URLs. Customers see a broken-link warning in the portal if a
  link is missing or returns an error.
</Tip>
