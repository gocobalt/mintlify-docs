**Native** is Refold's product for in-app integrations: your customers connect Salesforce, Workday, NetSuite, and 300+ other apps without leaving your product. You ship a branded connect experience using the embedded SDK and the Connect Portal, Refold handles the authentication and execution, and every customer's data stays scoped to their linked account.

Use Native when integrations are a product feature your customers turn on themselves. For one-off enterprise integration projects that you scope and deliver, use [Integration Delivery](/v3/developer/products/integration-delivery) instead.

## What you can do

- **Embed the connect portal** in your product so customers authenticate inside your UI, not on a Refold-hosted page.
- **Brand the experience** with your colors, logo, redirect URLs, and policy links from [Connect Portal](/v3/platform/platform-settings/workspace-settings/connect-portal) settings.
- **Configure integrations per customer**: enable specific workflows, set field values, and manage credentials at the [linked account](/v3/platform/concepts/linked-account) level.
- **Use the SDK** in your frontend (React, JS) and backend (Node) to mount the portal, list linked accounts, trigger workflows, and listen for events.
