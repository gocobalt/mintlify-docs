A **connector** is a prebuilt integration to a third-party application. Refold
maintains 300+ connectors across categories like CRM, Accounting, HRIS, ATS,
Sales, and Marketing, so you can offer an integration to your customers without
building against each provider's API yourself.

Every connector exposes two kinds of building blocks:

- **Actions** — operations Refold can perform on the app, such as creating a
  contact, reading records, or running a query. You call them directly or chain
  them inside a [workflow](/v3/concepts/workflows/overview).
- **Triggers** — events in the app that start a workflow, such as a record being
  created or updated.

The exact actions and triggers depend on the provider. Browse the full list per
app in the [connector catalogue](/v3/platform/guide/catalogue/overview).

## Enable a connector

Before a customer can connect an app, enable it in your workspace and configure
its credentials.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the app">
    In the dashboard's **Apps** section, select the application and click **Go
    Live**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure credentials">
    Open the app's **Settings** and provide what its auth method needs. For
    OAuth apps, supply your **Client ID** and **Client Secret** (or use Refold's
    credentials while testing). For API-key apps, no dashboard setup is
    required — your customer enters their key during the auth flow.

    See [Auth configs](/v3/platform/concepts/connector/authentication) for
    the full setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Match the scopes">
    Confirm the scopes listed under **Permissions & scopes** are also selected
    in your developer account on the provider's side.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
Your customers can now connect this app from the [auth
flow](/v3/platform/authentication/connection-flows/hosted-flow) and use its actions and
triggers.
</Check>

## When the catalogue isn't enough

- Need an action a connector doesn't ship with? Define your own — see [Custom
  actions](/v3/platform/concepts/connector/custom-actions).
- Need an app Refold doesn't list yet? Build it as a custom app.
