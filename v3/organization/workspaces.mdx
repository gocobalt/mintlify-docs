A **workspace** is an isolated Refold environment: its own integrations,
linked accounts, users, and settings. Use multiple workspaces when you need
hard separation between products, customers, or environments (production
versus staging), and keep one as your **primary** for billing and
single-sign-on routing.

Open the workspace switcher from the workspace name in the bottom-left of the
dashboard, or jump directly to the listing page.

## All workspaces

The **All workspaces** page shows every workspace you have access to as a
card grid. Each card carries the workspace name, type, environment, region,
seat count, and a switch action.

### What each field means

| Field | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Type** | The workspace category: `ISV / SaaS Provider` or `Enterprise Customer`. The type controls which Refold features are available. |
| **PRIMARY** tag | Marks the workspace Refold treats as your account's default for billing and SSO routing. There's exactly one primary. |
| **ENV** | The deployment environment (`Production`, `Staging`). |
| **REGION** | The AWS region the workspace is hosted in (`us-east-1`). |
| **SEATS** | The number of user seats the workspace's plan allows. |

## Switch workspaces

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open All workspaces">
    Click the workspace name in the bottom-left corner of the dashboard, then
    select **All workspaces**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Switch to Workspace">
    On the card for the workspace you want, click **Switch to Workspace**.
    Refold reloads the dashboard scoped to that workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your current workspace shows **Current Workspace** on its card instead of a
switch link.

## Create a workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Create card">
    On **All workspaces**, click the **+ Create Workspace** button in the
    top-right, or click the **Create new workspace** card at the end of the
    grid.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a type and environment">
    {/* NEEDS INPUT: list the exact fields in the Create Workspace form
        (name, type, region, environment, seat allocation, billing). */}
    Set the workspace **Name**, **Type**, **Region**, and **Environment**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Click **Create**. Refold provisions the workspace and adds it to your
    listing. Switch into it to start configuring.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Creating a workspace allocates seats from your plan. Confirm seat
  availability before you start.
</Note>

## Primary workspace

The **PRIMARY** tag marks the workspace Refold uses as your account's
default. It's the workspace billing belongs to, and it's the one SSO
routes to when no specific workspace is named in the sign-in URL.

## Workspace overview settings

The **Overview** page holds the high-level metadata for a workspace: its
display name, the URL slug used in dashboard links, the workspace ID used in
API calls, the contact email, the logo, and the subscription tier. Open it
from **Settings > Organization > Overview**.

### Edit workspace details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Overview">
    Go to **Settings > Organization > Overview**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update fields">
    Edit any of **Name**, **Website**, or **Logo** (paste a public image URL).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. Changes apply across the workspace immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Fields explained

| Field | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Display name shown in the dashboard, in invitation emails, and on the workspace switcher. |
| **Website** | Public URL of your company. Used in customer-facing flows like the Connect Portal. |
| **Email** | Workspace contact address. Refold sends billing and admin notifications here. To change it, click **Contact support**. |
| **Logo** | Public URL of an image used as the workspace avatar wherever the workspace name appears. |
| **Slug** | URL-safe identifier used in dashboard URLs (`refold-8301`). Read-only; copy it with the icon at the right of the field. |
| **ID** | Internal workspace identifier used in API requests and webhook payloads. Read-only; copy with the icon at the right of the field. |

### Subscription tier

The badge under the workspace logo in the right column shows your tier
(for example, **Enterprise**). To change tiers, contact your account
representative or use **Contact support** under the email field.
